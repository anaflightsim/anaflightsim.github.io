Flight Planned Routes

Find the right flight planned route to match the realistic operation in Japan is uneasy. The Auto Route Finder usually messes things up (e.g. cannot connect to SID or STAR, using an unauthrozied airway).

However, the editors of this website have worked hard comparing information from different sources (e.g. flightaware, flightradar24, and eAIP Japan (AIC 025/20) to find the most realistic routes for you.
